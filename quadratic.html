Intermediate Algebra: Conquering Quadratic Equations

Standard Form: \(ax^2 + bx + c = 0\) where \(a \neq 0\)

1. Method One: Solving by Factoring

Factoring is your first and fastest tool. It relies on the Zero Product Property (ZPP): If \(A \cdot B = 0\), then either \(A=0\) or \(B=0\).
